The Workflow

How collaboration works

Seamless integration from concept to fabrication.

Your External Partner

TheSteelDesigner.eu acts as your external steel design, analysis, Tekla and consultancy partner. The practice supports in-house teams when workload is high or when deeper expertise is needed in Eurocodes, SAP2000/ETABS analysis and structural behaviour.

Flexible Integration

We can join at almost any stage of a project – from early concept and global analysis to detailed Tekla modelling, consultancy on existing designs and second opinions before fabrication. The approach is simple: clear assumptions, clear calculations, clear deliverables.

Why Collaborate?

We fill specialist gaps in your team, focusing on technical excellence from global analysis to fabrication readiness, and offering independent review to ensure project success.

We build the validated FEM model, perform Eurocode checks, optimize sections, and synchronize the geometry.

03

Delivery

You receive comprehensive calculation reports, the 3D model, PDF drawings, and fabrication-ready NC files.
